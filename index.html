Creating Software for Nonprofits

Hack the Future is a student organization at Purdue aimed towards creating software for local nonprofits. We develop several projects throughout the school year with various different nonprofits with the hopes of bettering the community.

We are actively involved with the community and provide software development services at no cost to nonprofits! Hack the Future is comprised of students all working together to develop amazing projects.
